Exploring the life and work of Ila sachani.

Ila Sachani is a talented Kathiawar embroidery artist.She overcame physical challenges to excel in her creft.She does embroidery using her feet.Let's know more about her life and work.....
Ila's journey began in the quiet settings of Amreli,Gujarat,where she was born in a farmers family.From the begginning life presented challenges as Ila was born with her hanging loose by her sides.
Ila's mother and grandmother were focused to overcome her challenge.They were skilled with the traditional art of Kathiawar enbroidery and decided to share this art with Ila.They taught her to create many beautiful patterns using her feet.It was not an easy task but Ila accepted with determination.
Her parents also her to eat and comb her hair using her feet.
She faced many difficulties while learning it,but she didn't stop learning.She then slowly learned many other styles of enbroidery which were even out of Gujarat.

For Ila embroidery became a way to express herself.
First she made designs only for her friends and family who were amazed to see her work.Slowly,the people from her village discovered Ila's skill and admiration began to spread.
The news reached the government office in Surat . They were shocked by listening to Ila's story and were impressed by her work.
So,they decided to display some samples of her embroidery at a state exhibition.
After seeing that people are liking her work,she participated in the exhibitions at national level.

Because of her honor,creativity and will power she recieved a medal from the president.She proved through her creations yhat challenges can be turned into victories through focus and never giving up. Ila Sachani also teaches us that where there is a will there is a way.
